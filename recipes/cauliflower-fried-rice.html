CAULIFLOWER FRIED RICE

Cauliflower fried rice

Cauliflower fried rice is a low-carb alternative, using grated cauliflower instead of rice, with veggies
and protein stir-fried for a tasty, healthy dish.

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 small head of cauliflower or 1 (10- to 12- oz.) bag pre-riced cauliflower
  • 3 tbsp. reduced-sodium soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp. mirin
  • 1 tsp. granulated sugar
  • 1/4 c. neutral oil
  • 2 shallots, thinly sliced
  • 2 large eggs, beaten to blend
  • 1 carrot, cut into 1/4 matchsticks
  • 3 cloves garlic, finely chopped
  • 1/2 c. frozen peas
  • Kosher salt
  • freshly ground black pepper
  • 2 scallions, thinly sliced
  • Sesame seeds, for serving (optional)

STEPS

  1. Cut cauliflower into florets.
    In a food processor, pulse florets until rice-sized pieces form. (skip this step if using pre-riced
    cauliflower.)
    Place cauliflower in a clean kitchen towel.squeeze out excess moisture. (Do this with pre-riced
    cauliflower as well to ensure rice is dry.)
  2. In a small bowl, stri soy sauce, mirin, and sugar; set aside.
  3. In a large nonstrick skillet or work over medium-high heat, heat oil until just shimmering.
    Add shallots and cook, stirring occasionally until golden brown, 1 to 2 minutes.
    Transfer shallots to a papper-towel lined plate.
    Discard all but 1 tablespoon oil from skilet.
  4. In same skillet over medium-high heat, cook eggs, stirring occasionally until scrambled about
    30 seconds.
    Transfer eggs to a small bowl.
  5. In same skilet over medium-high heat, cook carrots, garlic, and peas, stirring frequently until
    warmed through about 30 seconds.
    Add cauliflower and stir to combine. cook, stirring occasionally until vegetables are slightly softened,
    about 2 minutes.
  6. Increase heat to high. Add reserved soy sauce mixture and cook, stirring frequently about 1 minute.
    Stir in eggs; season with salt and pepper.
  7. Divide fried rice among small bowls. Top with shallots, scallions, and Sesame seeds (if using).
